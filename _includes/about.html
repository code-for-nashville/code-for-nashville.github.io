About

Code for Nashville is Nashville's new Code for America Brigade. We are volunteers who collaborate with Metro government to promote open data and build civic apps.

We are a growing organization and need members with all types of skills.

The best way to become involved is to attend our next hack night. We meet monthly at the Sevier Park Community Center.
